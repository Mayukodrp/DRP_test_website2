Roses & Cut Flowers Import Business

DRP Japan Branch is engaged in the import and distribution of premium roses and cut flowers from leading international growers. We specialize in sourcing high-quality blooms that meet the exacting standards of the Japanese market.

Our dedicated team works closely with growers in key producing regions to ensure consistent supply of fresh, long-lasting flowers. We handle the entire logistics chain from farm to customer, maintaining optimal cold chain management throughout.
