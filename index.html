About DRP

Trading is at the heart of DRP's business.

DRP International Ltd. is a multi-commodity trading & distribution business incorporated in 2016 in Ontario, Canada.

Our team is highly experienced, multi-lingual, and global. We originate physical transactions that support our customers' businesses in AMER (North, Central, South America), EMEA (Europe, Middle East, Africa), and APAC (Asia-Pacific) regions.

Our global network and logistics capabilities allow for sourcing of commodities in one location and deliver in another - via sea borne freight, barges, trucks, and rail.

Our storage and financing capabilities allows us to execute trades across time zones and delivery periods. We endeavour to identify arbitrage opportunities with our extensive market knowledge and experience.

DRP has grown by creating value through building businesses from the ground up - powered by capital, relationships, and deep expertise.
